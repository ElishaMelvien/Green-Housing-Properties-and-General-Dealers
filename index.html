    Plot Booking Process

    We keep our process simple, transparent, and fair.

    1. Select Your Plot Choose an available plot from our listings or with assistance from our team.

    3. Plot Reservation The plot is secured in your name and removed from the market.
    4. Proceed to Purchase If you complete the purchase, the K800 deposit is deducted from the total plot price.
